# Project 03: Fintech AML Policy Review and Gap Analysis

## Project Overview

This fictional portfolio project demonstrates the review of an early-stage fintech company’s AML compliance policy and control framework.

The project assesses whether the existing policy adequately addresses key AML/KYC requirements and identifies areas requiring remediation.

## Scenario

A fictional fintech company provides digital payment accounts and cross-border transfer services.

The company has an existing AML policy, but management has requested an independent review before presenting the compliance framework to banking and payment-processing partners.

The review will evaluate the policy’s completeness, clarity, operational effectiveness and alignment with generally accepted risk-based AML principles.

## Review Areas

The assessment covers:

- AML governance and oversight
- Customer identification and verification
- Customer Due Diligence
- Enhanced Due Diligence
- Beneficial ownership
- Customer risk assessment
- Sanctions and PEP screening
- Transaction monitoring
- Suspicious-activity escalation
- Recordkeeping
- Staff training
- Quality assurance
- Policy review and approval
