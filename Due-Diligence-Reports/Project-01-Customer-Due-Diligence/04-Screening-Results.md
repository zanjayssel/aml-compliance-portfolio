## 5. Screening Limitations

The following limitations apply:

- The customer and business are fictional
- No real screening database was used
- No real adverse-media results were generated
- No real person or business was investigated
- The exercise demonstrates screening methodology rather than actual screening output

In a real compliance environment, screening would require:

- Approved sanctions and PEP screening tools
- Reliable open-source research
- Documented search parameters
- Review of aliases and alternative spellings
- Resolution of possible false positives
- Retention of supporting evidence and audit records
