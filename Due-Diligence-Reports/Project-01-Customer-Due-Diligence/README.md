# Project 01: Customer Due Diligence

## Project Overview

This portfolio project demonstrates a risk-based customer due diligence assessment involving a Namibian business owner seeking to open an account with a South African fintech company for cross-border commercial transactions.

The project assesses:

- Customer identity and verification requirements
- Nature and purpose of the business relationship
- Source of funds
- Geographic and cross-border risk
- Politically exposed person exposure
- Sanctions exposure
- Adverse media findings
- Overall customer risk
- Recommended onboarding controls

## Scenario

The prospective customer is an individual business owner residing in Namibia and operating a retail business in Windhoek. The customer wishes to open an account with a South African fintech company to make payments to South African suppliers.

Expected monthly transaction activity is between N$80,000 and N$120,000.

## Final Outcome

**Risk Rating:** Medium Risk

**Recommendation:** Approve subject to satisfactory completion of customer identification and verification, source-of-funds verification, and ongoing transaction monitoring.
